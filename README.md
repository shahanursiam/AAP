# AAP 

**AAP** is a comprehensive Garment Sample & Inventory Management System designed to streamline technical textile workflows. It facilitates the tracking of samples throughout their lifecycle—from prototype to production—while managing inventory movements, invoicing, and approvals.

## Key Features

### 👕 Sample Management
- Track samples by **Style No**, **PO Number**, **Season**, and **Fabric Details**.
- Support for various sample stages: `Proto`, `Fit`, `PP`, `Shipment`, `Production`.
- Barcode scanning for quick sample retrieval and status updates.

### 🏭 Inventory & Logistics
- **Location Tracking**: Monitor items across Warehouses, Offices, and specialized containers.
- **Movements**: Real-time logs of sample transfers between locations and users.
- **Container Management**: Organize samples into containers for bulk management.

### 💰 Merchandising & Finance
- **Invoicing**: Generate professional invoices with support for deductions, returns, and automatic calculations.
- **Approvals Dashboard**: Streamlined workflow for managers to approve sensitive transactions or shipments.
- **Reports**: Data-driven insights into sample turnover, factory performance, and defect rates.

### 👥 Role-Based Access
- **Admin**: Full system control and configuration.
- **Merchandiser**: Manage collections, buyers, and sample requests.
